Our Agricultural Mission

The St. Moritz Agriculture Ministry is dedicated to transforming farming practices in our community through sustainable agriculture, modern farming techniques, and comprehensive farmer education programs.

We believe that food security is fundamental to community development. Our programs focus on increasing crop yields, improving soil health, water conservation, and creating sustainable income opportunities for farming families.

  • Training farmers in improved seed varieties and planting techniques
  • Implementing efficient irrigation and water conservation systems
  • Promoting organic farming methods and soil health
  • Providing market access and fair trade opportunities
  • Offering microfinance and financial literacy training

Our Agriculture Programs

Crop Production

Training farmers in improved seed varieties, proper planting techniques, and sustainable crop rotation methods for higher yields including maize, beans, vegetables, and fruit cultivation.

Water Management

Implementing efficient irrigation systems and water conservation techniques including drip irrigation, rainwater harvesting, and soil moisture management for year-round production.

Organic Farming

Promoting chemical-free farming methods that protect soil health and produce nutritious, safe food through composting techniques, natural pest control, and organic certification.

Farmer Training

Comprehensive education programs covering modern farming techniques, business skills, and market access through weekly workshops, demonstration plots, and peer learning groups.

Market Access

Connecting farmers with buyers, establishing collection centers, and facilitating fair trade practices through cooperative formation, market linkages, and price negotiation support.

Microfinance

Providing small loans and financial literacy training to help farmers invest in improved seeds, tools, and farming infrastructure through savings groups and loan programs.
